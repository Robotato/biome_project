Government Implementation

We understand that large-scale change is necessary nationwide in order to progress public safety against sexual assault. Every initiative taken represents a step closer to our goal, and we believe that government implementation of our project or a similar iniative could be valuable in bringing together victims, caregivers and lawmakers. The United States is desperately in need of technological infrastructure to elucidate the complex and antiquated systems currently in place.

Easing Open Communication

Our project attempts to provide a resource for victims of sexual assault to easily establish connections with healthcare providers and government personnel, building personal confidence and federal accountability. It is much more than just a sexual assault test tracker; it aims to bring real conversation to a process riddled with miscommunication and a lack of transparency.
